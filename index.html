About me

My passion for programming started when I was accepted into LaunchCode's free coding bootcamp (Lc101) in 2018. After just a few classes, I knew that I wanted a career in software development. Post graduation, I maintained momentum by enrolling in another coding bootcamp. This time, the course was an offering from Washington University in Saint Louis and Trilogy Education. I graduated my second coding bootcamp then became a Teaching Assistant for LaunchCode during their 2019 and 2021 cohorts, in between which, I worked as an IT Consultant for Daugherty Business Solutions. Immediately after my second stint as a TA at LaunchCode, I became a Teaching Assistant for Southern Methodist University's Full Stack coding bootcamp.
